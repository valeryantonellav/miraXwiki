====== Networks ======

<WRAP info>
Networks connect miraX to public and proprietary monitoring systems.
</WRAP>

===== Global Networks =====

^ Network ^ Scope ^ Data Types ^
| Open-Meteo | Global | Forecasts, climate |
| NOAA | Global | Atmosphere, ocean |
| DAHITI | Global | Water levels |
| GEOGLOWS | Global | Streamflow forecasts |

===== Regional Networks =====

^ Network ^ Region ^ Data Types ^
| Waterinfo BL | Belgium | Hydrology |
| KNMI | Netherlands | Meteorology |

===== Available Functions =====

  * Browse stations
  * Plot time series
  * Query parameters
  * Use observations as model inputs
