====== Flooding and Rewetting ======

This tool simulates water spread over terrain.

===== Inputs =====

  * Digital Terrain Model (DTM)
  * Study area polygon
  * Water level points

===== Output =====

  * Flooded area map

{{ :toolboxes:mirax_flooding.png?960 |}}

===== Use Cases =====

  * Flood risk exploration
  * Scenario testing
