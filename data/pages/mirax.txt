====== miraX Wiki ======

Welcome to the official **miraX documentation wiki**.

miraX is a cloud-based decision-support platform designed to support monitoring, analysis, and management of water systems through geospatial information, time series, and integrated analytical tools.

Its purpose is to transform multiple sources of information into actionable insights for planning, operations, and risk management.

===== What is miraX? =====

miraX integrates multiple data sources into a unified environment, including:

  * Hydrological and meteorological datasets
  * Satellite observations
  * Forecast information
  * User-defined data

The platform enables users to:

  * Visualize geospatial information
  * Analyze time series
  * Compare scenarios
  * Support operational workflows

===== Digital Twin Concept =====

miraX supports digital-twin-based workflows by integrating observations, models, and geospatial information into a unified operational environment.

===== Core Components =====

==== Real-Time Monitoring ====

miraX integrates multiple observation sources to provide visibility into current system conditions, including:

  * Sensor networks
  * Satellite-derived information
  * Meteorological variables
  * Hydrological observations

These data streams support operational awareness and interactive visualisation.

----

==== Forecasting and Analysis ====

miraX supports analysis and scenario evaluation to assist operational and strategic decision-making.

----

==== Geospatial Intelligence ====

miraX provides integrated geospatial visualization and analysis capabilities, including:

  * Interactive spatial visualisation
  * Multi-layer data integration
  * Dynamic mapping tools

----

==== Scenario Simulation ====

The platform supports scenario-based workflows for planning and operational support.

Potential applications include:

  * Flood scenario analysis
  * Water allocation planning
  * Resilience assessments
  * Operational optimisation
